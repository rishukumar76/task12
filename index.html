Types & Applications of AI

Narrow AI

Specialized in one task, e.g., voice assistants, image recognition, chatbots.

General AI

Can perform any intellectual task like a human (still theoretical).

Super AI

More intelligent than humans (future concept, not yet achieved).

Voice Assistants

Examples: Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant help with daily tasks using AI.

Self-Driving Cars

AI enables vehicles to sense environment and drive safely with minimal human input.

AI in Healthcare

AI helps diagnose diseases, suggest treatments, and analyze medical data.

Interesting AI Facts

  • The term "Artificial Intelligence" was first coined in 1956.
  • AI is used in spam filters, recommendations, and even in your smartphone camera.
  • Machine Learning is a subset of AI that learns from data.
  • AI can beat world champions in chess and Go.
  • AI is expected to add $15 trillion to the world economy by 2030.
